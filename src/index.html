Don’t know if you had PPI?

If you took out a loan, credit card or mortgage in the last 15 years you could have a claim for mis-sold PPI. Think about all
the loans, credit cards and mortgages you have had, as they could well have had PPI added to them without you even knowing.

If you don't know whether you had PPI in most cases we can find out for you.

Not knowing they had PPI attached to their policy is a large contributing factor to a lot of our customers having had,
successful claims.

Usually we don't even need your credit card number or your loan agreement number, just the name of your bank or credit
card company to check if you had PPI.

If you don't have PPI, it won’t cost you a penny!
